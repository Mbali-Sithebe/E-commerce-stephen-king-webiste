The Talisman: 2024 Comeback Masterpiece Novel

Originally published in 1984, The Talisman is an epic fantasy thriller co-written by bestselling authors Stephen King and Peter Straub. Nominated for the World Fantasy Award and a Locus Award finalist in 1985, this gripping tale of a young boy's journey to save his mother continues to captivate readers. Soon to be adapted into a Netflix series, The Talisman remains a timeless classic.
